Generated sales insights for leading hardware company in India : Atliq Hardwares

  • Pooja Zambre

In this project, I have showcased my expertise in SQL and Power BI by generating valuable sales insights for Atliq Hardware, one of India's leading computer hardware producers. Utilizing a MySQL database, I connected and visualized the data through Power BI to create comprehensive and actionable dashboards.

Project Overview

The primary goal of this project was to provide the management team at Atliq Hardware with critical insights into their sales performance, helping them make data-informed decisions to optimize their business strategy and drive growth. The insights generated from this project are instrumental in identifying key areas for improvement, understanding market trends, and evaluating sales strategies.

Key Features of the Dashboard

  • Revenue Analysis:
    • Displaying total revenue, sales quantity, and profit margins over a selected period.
    • Visual indicators for quick identification of performance metrics against targets.
  • Market Contribution:
    • A bar chart illustrating the revenue contribution percentage by different markets, highlighting top-performing regions like Bhubaneshwar and Hyderabad.
    • Profit contribution by market to understand profitability variations across different regions.
  • Revenue Trend:
    • A line and bar chart combination showcasing revenue and profit margin trends over time, enabling the management to spot trends and seasonality in sales performance.
    • Monthly breakdowns to provide granular insights into how each market is performing over time.
  • Customer and Product Analysis:
    • Detailed tables listing top customers and products, their revenue contributions, and profit margins.
    • Insightful indicators showing how specific customers and products are contributing to overall sales and profitability.

Technical Implementation

  • Database Management:
    • Utilized MySQL to store and manage the sales data, ensuring data integrity and facilitating efficient data retrieval.
    • Performed complex SQL queries to extract, transform, and load data into Power BI.
  • Data Visualization:
    • Designed interactive and visually appealing dashboards in Power BI to present data in a meaningful way.
    • Implemented filters and slicers to allow users to dynamically explore the data and uncover deeper insights.

Impact

The dashboards created in this project empower the Atliq Hardware management team to:

  • Quickly assess overall business performance.
  • Identify high and low-performing markets.
  • Make informed decisions based on comprehensive data analysis.
  • Improve operational efficiency and strategic planning.

By leveraging my skills in SQL and Power BI, I have successfully transformed raw sales data into valuable insights, demonstrating my proficiency in data analysis and visualization. This project underscores my ability to understand complex business problems and provide actionable solutions through data-driven techniques.
